Advertising Information

This page explains how advertising works within the application and how user choices are respected.

Why Advertising is Used

Accessibility: Advertising helps keep the app free and accessible to all users.

Development: Ad revenue supports improvements, new features, and maintenance.

Sustainability: Advertising enables long-term support and updates.

Advertising ID

The application may use the Google Advertising ID, a user-resettable identifier, to support ad delivery.

  • Limit ad frequency
  • Show regionally relevant ads
  • Support optional personalization

The Advertising ID is not used with sensitive data, and user preferences are respected.

Personalized Advertising

Personalized ads are based on general usage signals to improve relevance.

  • Helps show relevant content
  • Improves user experience
  • Does not use sensitive personal data

User Control

Users can manage advertising preferences through app settings:

  • Enable or disable ads (if supported)
  • Enable or disable personalized ads
  • Access privacy and consent options

Privacy and Compliance

The application follows applicable privacy regulations and respects user consent.

  • Consent is requested where required
  • Users can withdraw consent anytime
  • No personal data is sold

Third-Party Services

Advertising may be provided by trusted partners who follow privacy standards.

Protection for Young Users

Additional safeguards are applied where required, including limiting personalized ads.

Ongoing Commitment

Advertising practices are regularly reviewed to ensure compliance and user trust.

Contact

For any questions regarding advertising, please contact the developer through the appropriate support channel.

Code of Conduct

Effective Date:March 12, 2026

This Code of Conduct outlines expected behavior for interactions related to this website, projects, and professional communication.

Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering a positive, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

All interactions should be inclusive and free from discrimination based on age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience, nationality, race, religion, or sexual identity.

Expected Behavior

  • Use welcoming and inclusive language
  • Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
  • Accept constructive criticism gracefully
  • Show empathy toward others
  • Use appropriate and respectful communication

Unacceptable Behavior

  • Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
  • Sharing private information without permission
  • Offensive or inappropriate language
  • Unwanted sexual attention or advances
  • Promoting illegal or harmful content
  • Hostile or disruptive behavior

Scope

This Code applies to all interactions related to the website, projects, communication channels, and professional presence.

Enforcement

Violations may result in actions such as warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans depending on severity.

Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it through the appropriate support or contact channel.

Guidelines

  • Correction: Minor issue with guidance provided
  • Warning: Formal notice for inappropriate behavior
  • Temporary Restriction: Limited access for serious violations
  • Permanent Restriction: Ban for repeated or severe violations

Attribution

This Code of Conduct is inspired by standard open-source community guidelines.

Enable Now Bar for Tomato on Samsung
This page contains instructions on how to enable Now Bar on Samsung devices for Tomato

Samsung has disabled Now Bar for external apps by default in OneUI 8.

To enable it for all apps (including Focus), first enable Developer Options: go into Settings > About phone > Software information > Tap on Build Number 7 times, then enter your password to continue.

You can then enable Now Bar for all apps by going into Settings > Developer Options > Live notifications for all apps
